About

My name is Konrad. ARNGCOR is my project - refined in every detail, with content developed together with movement and health specialists.

Konrad · SovinSky Studio

My name is Konrad, and I stand behind ARNGCOR and SovinSky Studio - my personal brand and independent project that I build from idea to finished app. This is not a large corporation with dozens of people on the team. It's me, my vision, and a product I work on with full commitment - because I believe daily movement practice can look different: simpler, smarter, and without building a plan from scratch.

The exercise catalogs were created with physical therapists, personal trainers, and yoga teachers. I assembled ARNGCOR from them - practice matching, the app, and the full user experience. The result is a tool that combines solid expertise with a thoughtful, polished digital product.

The ARNGCOR app

A mobile app for iOS (Android in development) with video training, challenge programs (Wheel, Front Split), Pain Relief sequences, and an optional Premium subscription in the Apple App Store.

Your daily practice is built from extensive exercise catalogs created with physical therapists, personal trainers, and yoga teachers. The app adapts it to your goal, flexibility level, and activity.

The app does not replace medical advice. Before exercising, if you have health concerns, talk to a specialist.
