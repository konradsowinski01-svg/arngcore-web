Features

Everything you need
for regular movement

You don't have to build the plan yourself. ARNGCOR assembles your daily practice from extensive exercise catalogs - created with physical therapists, personal trainers, and yoga teachers - and adapts it to your goal, level, and habits.

Daily video practice

A different, carefully built video practice every day - matched to your goal, age, activity, and flexibility level. Exercises from professionally prepared catalogs, without generative AI.

Progress tracking

Day streaks, minutes of work, and completed exercises in one place. You see real progress, not just the feeling that you worked out today.

Pain Relief

Short relief sequences for your neck, shoulders, and lower back when hours at a desk leave you stiff and tense.

Challenge programs

Step-by-step plans with a fixed base, strengthening exercises, and a checkpoint that opens the next level.

Profile and personalization

A short onboarding sets your goal, flexibility level (1-4), and training plan - without complicated settings.

Privacy first

No behavioral ads or third-party tracking analytics. Your data is yours. Privacy Policy

How it works

From first launch
to a daily habit

Four simple steps - ARNGCOR handles the rest.

Choose your goal

Strength and mobility, ease discomfort, new skills, or a bit of everything.

Flexibility test

A short test sets your level so the app knows where to start.

Daily practice

The app builds your practice from exercise catalogs. Video exercises have clear goals: hold the position, move smoothly.

Progress and challenges

Open the next stages, including Wheel and Front Split programs.

Challenges

Wheel and Front Split

Ambitious goals broken into thoughtful, achievable steps - with progress badges and a checkpoint before each new stage.

Step-by-step program

Wheel challenge

A gradual plan from basics to a full wheel. Each stage builds strength and mobility before you open the next, and you can track progress in your profile.

Step-by-step program

Front Split challenge

A structured stretching program for front and side splits. Strengthening exercises, a fixed base, and measurable progress in the app.
